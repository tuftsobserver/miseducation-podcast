(mis)Education at the SMFA

We asked students in the dual BA/BFA program about how being part of the SMFA has influenced their experiences as students and artists, on and off campus.

Maxine

“I wish I could walk into an art museum and point to an Asian-American artist and say ‘that’s my favorite artist!’”

Ava

“Art can act as a catalyst to injustices we see today”

Jady

“I need the balance”

Lona

“I have to come up with things to do, and do them”

Sasha

“It’s expanded what I consider myself to be capable of, both on a technical level and on a conceptual level”

Rachel

“I still haven’t found the perfect balance between the two (Economics and Art)”

Daniela

“You can keep working on something even when it’s finished”
